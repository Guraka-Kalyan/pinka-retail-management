# Pinaka Retail Management: Complete Business Guide

Welcome to the **Pinaka Retail Management System**, a specialized Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) platform built from the ground up for the meat retail and processing industry. 

This powerful tool bridges the gap between raw inventory intake, processing (dressing), branch-level sales, and bottom-line profitability. It ensures that business owners have 100% transparency into where their money is going and exactly how much they are earning across multiple storefronts.

---

## 🔄 The Core Business Workflow
The magic of Pinaka lies in its strict "Cost-to-Revenue Pipeline." The system tracks the complete lifecycle of your product so nothing is lost:

1. **Procurement (Supply):** You purchase raw inventory (e.g., full batches) from suppliers. The system logs the initial capital investment.
2. **Processing (Dressing):** Raw materials are processed into sellable forms (Bone, Boneless, Mixed). Pinaka calculates your "Packed Meat Value."
3. **Distribution (Shops):** Packed meat is assigned and dispatched to various branch locations.
4. **Operations (Daily Costs & Sales):** Shops record their daily operational expenses (salaries, packaging, repairs) and their daily sale revenues.
5. **Analytics (Net Profit):** Pinaka automatically deducts the raw purchase costs and the daily operational costs from your sales revenue to give you the exact **Net Profit**.

## 🏢 Key Modules & Sections

### 1. 📊 Analytics Dashboard (Main)
**The Command Center for Owners & Admins.**
*   **What it is:** A real-time executive dashboard summarizing the health of the entire business across all branches.
*   **Business Value:** Never guess your profit margins. The **Cost to Revenue Pipeline** clearly visualizes your *Total Packed Meat Value*, *Total Deductions*, *Total Sales Revenue*, and exactly how much *Net Profit/Loss* was generated overall.
*   **Shop Notes:** Quickly see operational notes pinned by shop managers (e.g., "Need battery repair," or "Billboard damaged") to stay on top of centralized maintenance.

### 2. 🚛 Central Inventory & Supply
**Where your investment begins.**
*   **What it is:** The ledger for raw material intake and distribution. Log every batch created or purchased.
*   **Business Value:** Tracks the exact capital leaving your bank account. It ensures that every kilo of meat purchased is accounted for in a "Central Vault" before it is assigned and dispatched to a retail shop.

### 3. 🔪 Dressing Module
**Where raw materials become sellable assets.**
*   **What it is:** A specialized processing module that allows workers to log the yield of raw batches into categorized categories: *Bone*, *Boneless*, and *Mixed*, along with their specific weights.
*   **Business Value:** Prevents stock shrinkage "in the back room." It calculates your true `Cost per kg` based on actual *Usable Meat* yield, ensuring you never accidentally price your retail goods below processing costs.

### 4. 🏪 Shop Management (Branch Operations)
**Decentralized storefront operations & micro-analytics.**
*   **Shop Overview & Micro-Analytics:** Instead of just looking at the company as a whole, managers can click into a specific shop to see a "Micro-Dashboard". It tracks the exact total stock weight currently sitting in their specific freezer, their local expenses, and their localized Net Result. It holds the branch manager strictly accountable for their location.
*   **Inventory In (External Procurement):** While shops usually get meat from the Central Supply, sometimes they have to buy emergency stock locally (e.g., from a nearby farm). This section allows the manager to log "External" stock. Doing so immediately deducts the purchase cost from their shop's daily profit margin, preventing hidden cash register spending.
*   **Preparation (Recipe Conversion):** Shops don't just sell raw meat—they often cook or prepare it. This tab allows staff to digitally convert 10kg of raw "Boneless" meat into 10kg of "Prepared Curry" or "Prepared Fry". This ensures your digital inventory always perfectly matches the physical reality of the fridge.
*   **Operational Costs (Petty Cash):** A dedicated ledger for daily expenditures like Labour, Ice, Transport, and Custom costs. By forcing managers to log their daily costs, the system prevents them from hiding high daily spending behind high sale volumes.

### 5. 💵 Sales Module
**The centralized execution hub for branch operations.**
*   **Sales Hub Structure:** The Sales module integrates Daily Costs, Counter Cash, and actual POS Sales entry under one roof for a specific branch (e.g., "Vaikuntapuram").
*   **Stock Availability Ledger ("Sales Records"):** Before submitting a sale, the cashier sees a live dashboard of "Total Available Stock & Preparation." You literally cannot over-sell stock that you do not physically have in the system.
*   **Detailed POS Entry:** Records not only what type of meat was sold (Bone, Boneless, Fry, Curry) but cleanly splits the *Payment Method* (Cash vs PhonePe) and *Discounts Given*. 
*   **Counter Cash Verification:** By separating "Cash Received" from digital "PhonePe Received," the system ensures that at the end of the day, the expected "Final Cash" exactly matches the physical cash sitting in the till's drawer.
*   **Business Value:** Instantly reduces the shop's physical inventory count while simultaneously recording the shop's revenue, creating an inescapable digital footprint (Daily Sales Log) for every single transaction.

### 6. 📈 Financial Reports
**Auditing, performance ranking, and accounting made simple.**
*   **Executive Insights & Shop Comparison:** Instantly see the "Top Performing Shop" vs. the "Highest Pending Shop" to identify sales bottlenecks or high-performers. The Shop Performance table groups all vital signs (Revenue, Operational Costs, External Costs) across all branches into one comparative view.
*   **Pending Inventory Summary & Alerts:** The system actively monitors inventory health. The "MODERATE" / "CRITICAL" / "TOP" status badges alert the owner to stores holding too much unsold meat or stores running dangerously low on stock. It even breaks down exactly how much *Bone* vs *Boneless* is pending.
*   **Global Recipe & Preparation Tracking:** A master view of all prepared goods (Fry/Curry) across the entire company. This allows owners to measure the demand for value-added cooked products versus raw meat.
*   **Consolidated Master Sales Log:** A raw chronological log of all retail transactions across all branches over any given date range. 
*   **Business Value:** It entirely removes the need for complex, manual Excel spreadsheets and instantly provides audited data ready for accounting, taxation, and executive decision-making.

### 7. 🔒 Roles & Security
*   **Admin Role:** Full access to all shops, analytics, and high-level inventory intake.
*   **Shop Manager/User Role:** Restricted access. A user assigned to "Tirupati Branch" can only see and manage operations, sales, and costs for Tirupati. They cannot view the profit margins or data of other shops, protecting corporate financial data.

## 🎯 Why Choose Pinaka?
Pinaka is not a generic POS system. It is uniquely tailored to the operational realities of retail businesses that involve **processing raw goods before selling them**. 

By tracking the exact cost of a batch from the supplier, adding the daily operational costs of running the shop, and subtracting it from the final retail sales—Pinaka gives owners the ultimate peace of mind: **Absolute Financial Transparency.**
